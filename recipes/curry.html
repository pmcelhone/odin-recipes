Curry

Description

This is a Japanese version of a dish originating in India. It could be spicy depending on the level you like.

Usually curry is made with a type of protein, carrots, potatoes, and onions. It's also meant to be eaten with white rice.

  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • protein
  • onions
  • curry seasoning block
  1. Cut veggies and meat into bite sized pieces.
  2. Fry meat and veggies until meat is half cooked.
  3. Add water and boil for 15 minutes.
  4. Turn off heat and mix in curry blocks until disolved.
  5. Serve with rice and enjoy!
