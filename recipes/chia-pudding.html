Chia Pudding

Description

Chia is a type of seed that when added to liquid thickens up the liquid.

Best made with a type of milk, it's super creamy, thick and delicious.

  • chia seeds
  • milk
  1. Measure out your chia seeds and milk.
  2. Mix to combine.
  3. Let sit in the fridge for desired consistency.
  4. Optional: add fruit or nut butter and enjoy!
